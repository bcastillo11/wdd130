Featured Rafts

Embark on an intense expedition through narrow canyons and aggressive rapids. This stretch of the “Colorado River” is designed for thrill-seekers and nature lovers alike. With Class IV–V rapids and towering canyon walls, it's an unforgettable adventure. Perfect for experienced rafting groups!

A perfect route for those who want to mix adventure with scenic beauty. This trip along the “Llano River” winds through a wide canyon with moderate flow and breathtaking desert views. Ideal for families with teens or adults in good shape looking for excitement without extreme intensity.

Enjoy a calm and peaceful ride along the “San Saba River.” This scenic float is perfect for families with young children or older adults. You’ll drift past lush trees, clear skies, and gentle waters. A relaxing way to soak in nature, safely and comfortably.
