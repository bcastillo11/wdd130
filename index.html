Texas

Texas is a large state in the southern U.S., bordered by Mexico, New Mexico, Oklahoma, Arkansas, Louisiana, and the Gulf of Mexico. It is the second-largest state in both size and population. Major cities include Austin (the capital), Houston, Dallas, and San Antonio. Texas is known for its cultural diversity, historic sites like the Alamo, and wide-open landscapes. A signature dish of the state is barbecue brisket, especially slow-cooked and richly seasoned.
